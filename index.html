Our History

Primex Hotels & Resorts has been providing luxurious hospitality experiences since 1985. Founded with the vision of creating unforgettable stays for discerning travelers, we have expanded to include beautiful properties in Sri Lanka's most stunning locations.

Our Trincomalee properties, overlooking the famous beaches of Nilaveli, Marble Beach, and Arasimalee, have won numerous awards for their sustainable design and exceptional service. We take pride in blending traditional Sri Lankan hospitality with modern luxury amenities.

The name "Primex" was chosen to represent our commitment to Prime Excellence in everything we do. From the quality of our accommodations to the personalized service we provide, every aspect of the Primex experience is designed to exceed expectations.

Over the years, we have hosted celebrities, royalty, and travelers from around the world, all seeking the unique Primex experience that combines natural beauty with unparalleled service. Our properties are designed to harmonize with their natural surroundings, offering guests an authentic experience of Sri Lanka's stunning coastal environments.

Today, Primex continues to set the standard for luxury beach resorts in Sri Lanka, with ongoing investments in sustainable tourism practices and community development initiatives that benefit the local areas surrounding our properties.

Our Beautiful Beaches

Nilaveli Beach

Famous for its soft white sands and crystal-clear waters, Nilaveli Beach is often ranked among the most beautiful beaches in the world. Our Primex Nilaveli property offers direct access to this pristine shoreline.

Marble Beach

Named for its smooth, marble-like stones that line the shore, Marble Beach offers a unique coastal experience. Our resort here provides stunning views of the natural harbor and surrounding hills.

Arasimalee Beach
